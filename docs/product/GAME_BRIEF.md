# Game Brief

## Working Title

Gravity Courier

## Genre

- Gravity-assist rocket game.
- Orbital path planning.
- Lightweight physics arcade.

## Core Loop

- Launch or steer the rocket.
- Approach a planet gravity well.
- Complete a readable lap around the planet.
- Exit the gravity well with a clear Transfer Boost.
- Travel farther and retry with a clearer plan.

## First Design Target

Make one completed planet lap and its follow-up Transfer Boost feel readable and satisfying.
